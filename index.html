About Me

Born in the Silicon Valley of India (Bangalore), I naturally grew an inclination towards computer science and technology. My love for developing user interfaces left me curious on how to design them.

Several attempts at design, 2 HCI conferences, 8 applications and a 21 hour flight later, I reached Ann Arbor, where I am currently learning how to design UX experiences at the University of Michigan. ​

Equipped with the marriage of my design and development skills (and lots of good food), I love solving social problems, and building fresh and beautiful interactive experiences. ​

I also love exploring new places (11 countries and counting) and make it a point to try the local cuisine at every place I visit.
